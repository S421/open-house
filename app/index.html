ABOUT

The world is in a constant state of change. Digital spaces too! With every new device, a new realm of possibilities emerges and redefines how we experience, understand and communicate with one another.

We, the students of IMM know first hand. Throughout the year our projects challenged us to think differently. From mocking up smart watch apps to developing full web applications, to creating VR simulations and experimenting with the physical web, we’ve designed and developed for a full stack of digital environments. Our work is done with user experience and inspiration in mind - and a lot of coffee.

Take a look at our profiles, get to know our stories, and explore our creations. We invite you to come to our open house to rediscover what it means to be on the web, and reimagine what is possible with interactive media. We’re just getting started.
